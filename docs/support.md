## What To Include / 提交问题时请包含

- App version and platform: iOS, Android, or web prototype.
- Device model and operating system version.
- Account email used for testing.
- Pet profile or conversation involved.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- Screenshots if they do not expose private medical records or precise home addresses.

- App 版本和平台：iOS、Android 或 Web 原型。
- 设备型号和系统版本。
- 测试账号邮箱。
- 相关宠物资料或会话。
- 复现步骤。
- 如截图不包含私密医疗记录或精确家庭地址，可附截图。

## Safety Reports / 安全举报

Use the in-app report, block, and unmatch flows for unsafe behavior. For internal testing, also email the support contact with the category: fraud, neglect, unsafe breeding, harassment, spam, or illegal sale.

遇到不安全行为，请优先使用 App 内举报、拉黑和解除匹配流程。内测阶段，也可以通过支持邮箱补充说明分类：欺诈、忽视照护、不安全繁育、骚扰、垃圾信息或非法售卖。

## Account Deletion / 账号删除

The app includes an account deletion request flow. During internal testing, deletion requests should include the testing email and a short reason so the workflow can be verified.

App 内已包含账号删除请求流程。内测阶段，删除请求应包含测试邮箱和简短原因，以便验证流程。
