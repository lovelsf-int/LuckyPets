# LuckyPets Terms Of Service Draft / 服务条款草稿

Last updated / 最后更新：2026-04-22

This is a working draft for internal testing and store preparation. Before public launch, review the final terms with qualified legal counsel and publish them at a public URL.

本文档是用于内测和商店准备的工作草稿。公开发布前，请由合格法律顾问审阅最终条款，并发布为公网 URL。

## Service / 服务内容

LuckyPets helps pet owners create pet profiles, browse compatible pets, match by intent, and start structured conversations for social introductions, playdates, and responsible breeding review flows.

LuckyPets 帮助宠物家长创建宠物资料、浏览合适的新朋友、按目的匹配，并围绕交友、玩伴和负责任繁育审核流程开启结构化沟通。

## Not A Human Dating App / 非真人约会应用

LuckyPets is not designed as a dating app for people. It is an owner-led pet social app focused on pet safety, owner consent, and animal welfare.

LuckyPets 不是面向人的约会应用。它是以家长为主导的宠物社交应用，重点关注宠物安全、家长同意和动物福利。

## Owner Responsibilities / 家长责任

Users are responsible for:

- Providing accurate pet and owner information.
- Following local laws and animal welfare rules.
- Supervising pets during any meetup.
- Avoiding precise home address disclosure in public profiles.
- Not uploading false, misleading, illegal, or harmful content.

用户需负责：

- 提供真实准确的宠物和家长信息。
- 遵守当地法律和动物福利规则。
- 在线下见面时照看好宠物。
- 避免在公开资料中披露精确家庭地址。
- 不上传虚假、误导、违法或有害内容。

## Responsible Breeding / 负责任繁育

Breeding conversations are restricted. Before breeding can be treated as approved, required evidence may include owner identity verification, vaccination records, parasite prevention records, age and body condition checks, vet records, and relevant genetic screening.

繁育沟通属于受限制流程。繁育被视为可推进前，可能需要家长身份核验、疫苗记录、驱虫记录、年龄与体况证明、兽医记录和相关基因筛查。

Users must not use LuckyPets for bulk breeding, sale-like listings, illegal animal trade, unsafe breeding pressure, or welfare-risk behavior.

用户不得将 LuckyPets 用于批量繁育、类似售卖的信息发布、非法动物交易、不安全繁育施压或动物福利风险行为。

## Safety And Moderation / 安全与审核

LuckyPets may provide reporting, blocking, unmatching, review queues, and breeding eligibility checks. Content or accounts may be restricted when they create safety, fraud, harassment, illegal sale, neglect, or animal welfare risks.

LuckyPets 可提供举报、拉黑、解除匹配、审核队列和繁育资格检查。当内容或账号造成安全、欺诈、骚扰、非法售卖、忽视照护或动物福利风险时，可能被限制。

## No Veterinary Or Legal Advice / 非兽医或法律建议

LuckyPets does not provide veterinary, breeding, or legal advice. Users should consult qualified veterinarians, animal welfare professionals, and legal advisors for decisions involving pet health, breeding, or compliance.

LuckyPets 不提供兽医、繁育或法律建议。涉及宠物健康、繁育或合规的决策，用户应咨询合格兽医、动物福利专业人士和法律顾问。

## Account Deletion / 账号删除

Users should be able to request account deletion in the app. Deletion may require identity verification and may retain limited records when needed for safety review, legal compliance, fraud prevention, or dispute handling.

用户应可在 App 内申请删除账号。删除可能需要身份验证；出于安全审核、法律合规、反欺诈或争议处理需要，可能保留有限记录。

## Changes / 条款变更

LuckyPets may update these terms as the product evolves. Material changes should be communicated through the app, website, or other appropriate channels before public launch.

随着产品演进，LuckyPets 可更新这些条款。公开发布前，重要变更应通过 App、网站或其他适当渠道告知用户。
