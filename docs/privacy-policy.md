# LuckyPets Privacy Policy Draft / 隐私政策草稿

Last updated / 最后更新：2026-04-22

This is a working draft for internal testing and store preparation. Before public submission, publish the final privacy policy at a public URL and review it with qualified legal counsel.

本文档是用于内测和商店准备的工作草稿。正式提交前，请将最终隐私政策发布为公网 URL，并交由合格法律顾问审阅。

## Product Scope / 产品范围

LuckyPets is a pet social matching app for pet owners. It supports pet profiles, social matching, playdate matching, responsible breeding eligibility gates, messages, reports, blocking, unmatching, and account deletion requests.

LuckyPets 是面向宠物家长的宠物社交匹配应用，支持宠物资料、交友匹配、玩伴匹配、负责任繁育资格门禁、消息、举报、拉黑、解除匹配和账号删除请求。

## Data We May Collect / 我们可能收集的数据

- Account data: email, display name, session information, account deletion request.
- Pet profile data: pet name, species, breed, age, sex, city or coarse area, personality, matching intent, owner notes.
- User content: photos, health record metadata, messages, reports, and safety action records.
- Matching and safety data: likes, passes, matches, blocked owners, unmatched conversations, report categories.
- Diagnostics: crash logs and basic analytics if enabled.

- 账号数据：邮箱、展示名、会话信息、账号删除请求。
- 宠物资料：宠物名、物种、品种、年龄、性别、城市或粗粒度区域、性格、匹配目的、家长备注。
- 用户内容：照片、健康记录元数据、消息、举报和安全操作记录。
- 匹配与安全数据：喜欢、跳过、配对、拉黑、解除匹配和举报分类。
- 诊断数据：启用后可能收集崩溃日志和基础分析。

## How We Use Data / 数据用途

- Provide account, pet profile, matching, messaging, moderation, and account deletion features.
- Protect animal welfare through reporting, blocking, unmatching, and breeding eligibility review.
- Improve app reliability and internal testing quality.
- Prepare store compliance disclosures for App Store and Google Play.

- 提供账号、宠物资料、匹配、消息、审核和账号删除能力。
- 通过举报、拉黑、解除匹配和繁育资格审核保护动物福利。
- 提高应用稳定性和内测质量。
- 准备 App Store 和 Google Play 的合规披露材料。

## Location And Medical Data / 位置与医疗数据

LuckyPets should use coarse location by default, such as city or district-level areas. Public pet profiles should not expose precise home addresses.

LuckyPets 默认应使用粗粒度位置，例如城市或区域。公开宠物资料不应展示精确家庭地址。

Health records and medical documents should be private by default. They should be shared only after explicit owner action or reviewed through a controlled moderation process.

健康记录和医疗文件默认应保持私密。只有在家长明确操作后，或通过受控审核流程时，才应被共享或查看。

## Sharing And Disclosure / 共享与披露

LuckyPets should not sell personal data. Data may be shared only when needed for app operation, safety review, legal compliance, fraud prevention, or user-directed actions.

LuckyPets 不应出售个人数据。只有在应用运行、安全审核、法律合规、反欺诈或用户主动操作所需时，才可能共享数据。

## User Controls / 用户控制

Users should be able to:

- Edit pet profile information.
- Request account deletion in the app.
- Report unsafe behavior.
- Block owners.
- Unmatch conversations.
- Avoid sharing precise home addresses or private medical documents in public profiles.

用户应能够：

- 编辑宠物资料。
- 在 App 内申请删除账号。
- 举报不安全行为。
- 拉黑家长。
- 解除匹配。
- 避免在公开资料中分享精确家庭地址或私密医疗文件。

## Retention / 保留期限

Data should be retained only as long as necessary for product operation, safety review, compliance, dispute handling, and internal testing. Account deletion requests should trigger deletion or anonymization workflows once verified.

数据仅应在产品运行、安全审核、合规、争议处理和内测所需期间保留。账号删除请求经验证后，应触发删除或匿名化流程。

## Children / 未成年人

LuckyPets is designed for pet owners and is not intended for children. If a store or jurisdiction requires a specific age threshold, the final policy and app settings should be updated before submission.

LuckyPets 面向宠物家长，并非儿童产品。如果商店或司法辖区要求具体年龄门槛，正式提交前应更新最终政策和 App 设置。
